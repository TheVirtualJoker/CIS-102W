About Us

The Esports Foundation, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charitable organization founded by philanthropic gamers as a way to “give back” from the esports community. With the continued growth of the competitive video game scene in the United States, we believe in the importance of a dedicated organization in esports working directly with professional teams, leagues, studios, and publishers on charitable efforts. We think we can make the highest impact to help children in need of assistance as outlined in our mission statement.
